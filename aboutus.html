Who we are?

We provide highly qualified professionals to support aerospace industry, employing experts in aircraft maintenance and repair. DHAR AIR has a dynamic culture focused on bringing together the right talent team to deliver solutions that help our customers across Canada, operate more efficiently.


Our leadership

We've been working with MRO's and aircraft manufacturing companies in Mexico, USA and Canada for the past 21 years. Working directly and actively in the industry, we know the required skills that maintenance personnel must have to guarantee the operation and safety of the aircraft, to meet the standards of our clients, which set us apart from recruiters who do not understand the specific needs of our industry.

David Aguilar
Founder and Director

Under his leadership, DHAR AIR INC’s focus is to help our staff achieve their personal and professional goals, helping them feel empowered, respected and creating a skilled, inclusive and diverse workforce, which is reflected in the service that we provide to our customers.


David Aguilar

David Aguilar has been provided maintenance, repair and overhaul services in the aeronautical industry for over 21 years, in Mexico, USA and Canada. Mr Aguilar holds a bachelor’s degree in Control and Automation Engineering, the FAA A&P Licensed and an Aircraft Mechanic Diploma in Canada. His experience is based on performing mechanical troubleshooting and maintenance on heavy checks, inspections, modifications, testing, overhaul maintenance, A checks and C checks on ATR72, DASH-8, B737, B747, B757, B767, B787, A-320 family, A-330, Embraer 175, Embraer 190 aircraft. He worked from the bottom up starting as an apprentice Aircraft Mechanic and later he was promoted to Aircraft Team Leader, always focus to work efficiently and safely, providing technical solutions.

Rocio Bautista

She is committed to benefiting both aerospace companies and professionals alike. Aeronautical companies benefit from having the best professionals in the field who will focus their potential and skills on providing service that pushes to finish aircraft maintenance and manufacturing in a timely manner and our staff benefits from finding the ideal job, where they can apply their extensive knowledge and skills, enjoy their profession and have personal and professional growth.

Rocio Bautista

Rocio Bautista holds a bachelor’s degree in Control and Automation engineering, a technical degree in Metrology and Quality Control, and a Diploma in Aircraft Mechanic in Canada. Rocio has over 13 years of experience in aviation industry, she has got experience working in Avionics Systems on ATR72, DASH-8, B737, B747, B757, B767, B787, A-319, A-320, Embraer 175, Embraer 190, MD-11 and DC-9 Aircrafts. As Quality Control Inspector in a Metrology Laboratory, performing calibration and preventive maintenance on measuring instruments used in aircraft, also obtained training in Non-Destructive Eddy Current Testing Level 1. She enjoys and loves her profession and is constantly updating herself in the business world and focusing her knowledge to benefit the aeronautical field.
